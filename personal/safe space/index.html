    MY MENTAL HEALTH

    I am currently diagnosed with PTSD, schizoaffective disorder, and major depression. I have had a rough life, but I am genuinely happy to still be here and still in recovery. And I am even happier to be with my sis creating things together. She has been the best thing that has ever happened to me in this world. Sun is someone sensitive and sweet! and she is talented. I watched her grow up and I am proud of that. There are days when even with my meds I still feel pretty sad and down, but she is always there to cheer me up.

    And of course I have Midnight! She is my emotional support animal and she has helped me a lot these years, she has given me peace and calm and she has also brought me happiness. I always recommend everyone to have an emotional support animal, if you can get one, definitely don't miss that opportunity! I'm sure that a puppy or a kitten will help you improve your mental health. They are such beautiful and wonderful beings that the happiness they give you is priceless.
