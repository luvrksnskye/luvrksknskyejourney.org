The Celestial Foodie



The plump and happy constellation of Gordo spreads across the western horizon like a well-fed guardian. His stars form the shape of a chubby, lounging dog with a perpetually wagging tail. According to myth, Gordo was a baker's dog who would taste-test all the bread to ensure it was perfect, leading to his rounded figure. The gods were so charmed by his dedication to quality control that they honored him with a place among the stars. It's said that bakers who start their ovens when Gordo's constellation is brightest will have their bread rise perfectly.
