You have found an Yogui star fragment! Wow! The stars of Yogui pulse in patterns matching meditation rhythms, and this constellation contains the oldest known paw-shaped nebula... Emits calming waves of light during full moons and shows perfect symmetry during solstices!

Did you also know that this constellation is the sister of the constellations Gordo and Cachi? Together they form what some astronomers call "The three dogs of the sky".

The Wise Observer



Rising in the eastern sky, Yogui appears as a wise dog sitting in a contemplative pose. The stars that form his figure tell the story of a philosophical canine who could understand the language of birds and would share their wisdom with lost travelers. The brightest star in his constellation, marking his heart, is said to pulse with ancient knowledge. Mountain hermits claim that meditation under Yogui's stars grants deeper understanding of life's mysteries.

However, it is a very small puppy constellation! Isn't it curious?
