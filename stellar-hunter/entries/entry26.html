Guardian of Dreams



Deep in the night sky prowls Midnight, a constellation of a sleek cat with stars so dark they seem to absorb the light around them. Stories tell of a cat who could walk between worlds, guarding the boundary between day and night. Her eyes, formed by two bright blue stars, are said to glow brightest during the late night. Night watchmen believe that seeing Midnight's constellation brings extra alertness during their darkest hours.
