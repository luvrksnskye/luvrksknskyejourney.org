The Sweet Spirit



The sweet-natured Mochi appears in the southern sky, her stars arranged in the shape of a plump, contented cat. Legend says she was the beloved pet of a Sweet shop, helping to taste-test desserts and spreading sweetness throughout the world. Her constellation is said to sparkle like sugar crystals, and pastry chefs swear their creations turn out better when Mochi's stars shine through their kitchen windows.
