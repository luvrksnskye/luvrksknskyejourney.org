The Great Entertainer



Strutting across the autumn sky, Filomeno's stars form the shape of a cat in a perpetually dramatic pose. Ancient stories tell of a theatrical feline who could change the weather with his various moods and poses. The gods found his dramatic nature so entertaining that they gave him a permanent stage among the stars. Artists say that when Filomeno's constellation is bright, their creative inspiration flows more freely.
